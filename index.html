Excel Format Guide

Your Excel file should include these columns:

Required columns:
tracking_id, patent_title, patent_applicant, client_id
Optional columns:
patent_type, application_no, date_of_filing, applicant_address,
inventor_email, inventor_phone, professional_fees, reimbursement,
notes, priority, inventors

Patent types: PS_ONLY, CS_ONLY, PS_TO_CS

Priority levels: low, normal, high, urgent
